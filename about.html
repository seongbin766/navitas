Navitas Performance Center

Recruit------>Train------>Designer

Hello, I'm Seongbin Lee, the founder of Navitas. The Navitas Performance Center in Shanghai, China, is the training ground for Navitas's next generation of designers. The state of the art-training facility is designed to develop the well-rounded designers on and off store, provding recruits to the opportunity to learn under a world class coaching team as they work toward their dream of becoming Navitas Designers.

The Journey of a Navitas Designer starts here.

This place is for finalist recruits to train hard, just to have the opportunity to be a top Navitas designer that we currently have.

Warning

Here is one warning. When picking recruits for the tryouts, the training will be hard. This training center is without rest. If the tryouts are hard, then the peformance center will too. If people don't try their best, we kick them out one by one and this is a big challenge for the local people out there.
